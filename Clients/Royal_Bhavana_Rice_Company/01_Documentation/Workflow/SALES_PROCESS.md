\# SALES PROCESS



\## Purpose



This document defines the standard sales process followed by Royal Bhavana Rice Company for handling customer enquiries, nurturing leads, and converting them into customers.



The objective is to create a consistent and measurable sales workflow.



\---



\# Sales Process Overview



Lead Received



↓



Lead Assigned



↓



Initial Contact



↓



Requirement Understanding



↓



Product Recommendation



↓



Customer Follow-up



↓



Order Confirmation



↓



Delivery Coordination



↓



Customer Feedback



↓



Relationship Management



\---



\# Step 1 – Lead Received



Lead Sources



\- Website

\- Facebook

\- Instagram

\- WhatsApp

\- Google Business Profile

\- Phone Call

\- Referral



Action



System records the lead automatically.



Target Time



Immediate.



\---



\# Step 2 – Lead Assignment



Responsible



Sales Executive



Action



Review new lead and begin contact.



Target Time



Within 30 minutes during business hours.



\---



\# Step 3 – Initial Contact



Objective



Understand customer needs.



Collect



\- Product Requirement

\- Quantity

\- Location

\- Purpose (Home / Retail / Distributor)



Update CRM remarks.



\---



\# Step 4 – Product Recommendation



Sales Executive should recommend suitable products based on:



\- Customer requirement

\- Product availability

\- Business type



Provide relevant purchase links where applicable.



\---



\# Step 5 – Follow-up



Communication Channels



\- Phone Call

\- WhatsApp

\- Future: Email



Every interaction should be recorded in CRM.



\---



\# Step 6 – Order Confirmation



If customer is ready:



\- Confirm product

\- Confirm quantity

\- Confirm delivery details

\- Share purchase information



Update CRM status to:



Converted



\---



\# Step 7 – Delivery Coordination



Coordinate with the appropriate distribution or sales channel.



Update customer if required.



\---



\# Step 8 – Customer Feedback



After successful purchase:



\- Request customer feedback

\- Encourage Google Review

\- Encourage repeat purchase



\---



\# Step 9 – Relationship Management



Maintain long-term engagement through:



\- Festival greetings

\- Product updates

\- New product launches

\- Promotional offers



\---



\# Sales KPIs



Track:



\- Total Leads

\- Contact Rate

\- Qualification Rate

\- Conversion Rate

\- Average Response Time

\- Average Conversion Time

\- Repeat Customers

\- Best Lead Source



\---



\# Standard Response Time



| Activity | Target |

|----------|---------|

| Lead Assignment | Within 30 minutes |

| First Contact | Within 2 hours |

| Follow-up | As scheduled |

| CRM Update | Immediately after interaction |

\# Success Criteria



Every enquiry should:



\- Receive a timely response

\- Be tracked until closure

\- Have complete interaction history

\- Be measurable through reports



The goal is to maximize customer satisfaction while improving sales efficiency.
