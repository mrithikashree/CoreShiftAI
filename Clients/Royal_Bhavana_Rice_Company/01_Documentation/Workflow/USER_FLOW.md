\# USER FLOW



\## Purpose



This document explains how different users interact with the Royal Bhavana Rice Company Lead Management System.



\---



\# User Types



The system has the following users:



1\. Website Visitor

2\. Sales Executive

3\. Manager

4\. System Administrator



\---



\# Website Visitor Flow



Website Visitor



↓



Visits Landing Page



↓



Views Products



↓



Reads Product Benefits



↓



Views Testimonials



↓



Clicks "Enquire Now"



↓



Fills Google Form



↓



Receives Thank You Message



↓



Waits for Sales Team Contact



\---



\# Sales Executive Flow



Sales Executive



↓



Login



↓



Dashboard



↓



View Assigned Leads



↓



Open Lead Details



↓



Call Customer



↓



Update Lead Status



↓



Add Follow-up Notes



↓



Schedule Next Follow-up



↓



Convert Lead



OR



Mark as Lost



\---



\# Manager Flow



Manager



↓



Login



↓



Dashboard



↓



Monitor All Leads



↓



Assign Leads



↓



View Team Performance



↓



View Conversion Reports



↓



Export Reports



\---



\# System Administrator Flow



Administrator



↓



Login



↓



Manage Users



↓



Manage Roles



↓



Manage Products



↓



Manage Categories



↓



System Backup



↓



Review Logs



↓



Configure Integrations



\---



\# Access Matrix



| User | Permissions |

|------|-------------|

| Website Visitor | View website, Submit enquiry |

| Sales Executive | Manage assigned leads |

| Manager | View all leads, Reports |

| Administrator | Full system access |
