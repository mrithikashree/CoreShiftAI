\# Detailed Screen Flow



\## Step 1 - Landing Website



Customer visits the Royal Bhavana Rice Company website.



Customer can:



\- View Products

\- Read Benefits

\- Watch Videos

\- View Testimonials

\- Contact Company

\- Submit Enquiry



↓



\## Step 2 - Product Enquiry



Customer clicks:



Enquire Now



↓



Google Form opens.



Customer enters:



\- Name

\- Mobile Number

\- City

\- Product Interested

\- Quantity

\- Message



↓



Submit



↓



\## Step 3 - Lead Capture



System stores enquiry inside Google Sheets.



Lead Status



New



↓



\## Step 4 - CRM Dashboard



Sales Executive logs into CRM.



Dashboard displays



\- Total Leads

\- New Leads

\- Warm Leads

\- Hot Leads

\- Converted Leads



↓



Executive opens Lead Details.



↓



\## Step 5 - Follow-up



Executive contacts customer.



Status changes:



New



↓



Contacted



↓



Interested



↓



Quotation Shared



↓



Negotiation



↓



Converted



OR



Closed Lost



↓



\## Step 6 - Reports



Manager views



\- Daily Leads

\- Monthly Leads

\- Source Reports

\- Conversion Reports

\- Sales Performance
