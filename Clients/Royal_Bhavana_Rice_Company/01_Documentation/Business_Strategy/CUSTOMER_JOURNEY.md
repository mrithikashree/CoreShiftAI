\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\# Customer Journey



\---



\# Purpose



This document describes the end-to-end customer journey from discovering Royal Bhavana Rice Company to becoming a loyal customer.



The objective is to design a digital experience that maximizes trust, enquiries, and online purchases.



\---



\# Customer Journey Overview



Awareness



↓



Interest



↓



Consideration



↓



Action



↓



Follow-up



↓



Purchase



↓



Repeat Purchase



↓



Referral



\---



\# Stage 1 – Awareness



Customer discovers Royal Bhavana Rice Company through:



\- Facebook

\- Instagram

\- Google Search

\- Google Business Profile

\- Friends \& Family

\- QR Code

\- YouTube

\- Amazon

\- Blinkit

\- Jiomart

\- Zepto

\- Swiggy Instamart



Business Goal



Create curiosity.



\---



\# Stage 2 – Interest



Customer visits the Landing Page.



Customer wants answers to:



\- What is Royal Bhavana Rice?

\- Why is it called Sugar Free Rice?

\- Is it healthy?

\- Who can consume it?

\- What products are available?

\- Where can I buy it?



Business Goal



Generate interest.



\---



\# Stage 3 – Trust Building



Landing Page should build confidence using:



\- Company Story

\- Product Benefits

\- Certifications (if available)

\- Customer Testimonials

\- Videos

\- Google Reviews

\- Frequently Asked Questions



Business Goal



Reduce buying hesitation.



\---



\# Stage 4 – Decision



Customer chooses one of the following:



Option A



Buy Online



↓



Amazon



↓



Blinkit



↓



Jiomart



↓



Zepto



↓



Swiggy Instamart



OR



Option B



Click WhatsApp



↓



Talk to Sales Team



OR



Option C



Submit Enquiry Form



↓



Lead Generated



Business Goal



Increase conversions.



\---



\# Stage 5 – Follow-up



Telecaller contacts customer.



Activities:



\- Explain Products

\- Understand Requirement

\- Recommend Product

\- Record Notes

\- Schedule Follow-up



Business Goal



Convert enquiries into customers.



\---



\# Stage 6 – Purchase



Customer purchases product.



Possible Channels



\- Amazon

\- Retail Store

\- Distributor

\- Direct Sales



Business Goal



Complete sale.



\---



\# Stage 7 – Retention



Customer receives:



\- Product Updates

\- Health Tips

\- New Launches

\- Festival Offers



Business Goal



Encourage repeat purchases.



\---



\# Stage 8 – Advocacy



Satisfied customers:



\- Leave Google Reviews

\- Share on Social Media

\- Recommend to Family

\- Refer Friends



Business Goal



Generate organic growth.



\---



\# Customer Experience Principles



\- Build trust first.

\- Educate before selling.

\- Make contacting easy.

\- Provide multiple buying options.

\- Reduce customer effort.

\- Create long-term relationships.
