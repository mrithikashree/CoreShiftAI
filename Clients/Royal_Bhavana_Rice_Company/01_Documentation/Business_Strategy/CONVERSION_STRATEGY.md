\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\# Conversion Strategy



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the conversion objective for every section of the Royal Bhavana Rice Company Digital Sales \& Lead Management Platform.



The goal is to maximize customer trust, enquiries, and online purchases.



\---



\# Primary Business Goals



1\. Generate Product Sales

2\. Generate Qualified Leads

3\. Increase Brand Trust

4\. Encourage Repeat Purchases



\---



\# Landing Page Conversion Strategy



\## Section 1 – Hero Banner



Purpose



Capture attention within the first 5 seconds.



Customer Questions



\- What is this product?

\- Why should I care?



Primary CTA



\- Buy Online

\- Enquire Now

\- WhatsApp Us

\# Success Metrics



Measure:



\- Landing Page Visitors

\- WhatsApp Clicks

\- Buy Online Clicks

\- Lead Form Submissions

\- Phone Calls

\- Google Maps Clicks

\- Repeat Customers



\---



\# Conversion Principles



\- Educate before selling.

\- Build trust before asking for action.

\- Offer multiple buying options.

\- Reduce friction.

\- Keep calls-to-action visible.

\- Make every section support a business goal.
