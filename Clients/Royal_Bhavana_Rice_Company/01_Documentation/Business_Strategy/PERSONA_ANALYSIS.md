\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\# Customer Persona Analysis



\---



\# Purpose



This document identifies the primary customer personas for the Royal Bhavana Rice Company Digital Sales Platform.



Understanding customer personas helps us design a better user experience, stronger messaging, and higher conversion rates.



\---



\# Persona 1 – Retail Family Buyer



\## Profile



\- Age: 28–55

\- Family-oriented

\- Purchases rice for household consumption



\## Goals



\- Buy quality rice

\- Good taste

\- Reliable brand

\- Easy ordering



\## Pain Points



\- Too many brands available

\- Unsure about quality

\- Wants trusted recommendations



\## Preferred Actions



\- View products

\- Read reviews

\- Buy online

\- Contact via WhatsApp if needed



\---



\# Persona 2 – Health-Conscious Consumer



\## Profile



\- Age: 30–65

\- Interested in healthy eating

\- May search for diabetic-friendly or low GI options



\## Goals



\- Understand product benefits

\- Learn about nutritional value

\- Make informed purchasing decisions



\## Pain Points



\- Confusing product claims

\- Lack of trustworthy information



\## Preferred Actions



\- Read health information

\- Explore FAQs

\- Watch videos

\- Contact for clarification



\---



\# Persona 3 – Distributor / Wholesale Buyer



\## Profile



\- Retailer

\- Distributor

\- Supermarket

\- Institutional Buyer



\## Goals



\- Reliable supply

\- Business partnership

\- Bulk purchasing



\## Pain Points



\- Availability

\- Pricing discussions

\- Support responsiveness



\## Preferred Actions



\- Submit business enquiry

\- Call sales team

\- Request distributor information



\---



\# Common Expectations



Regardless of persona, every visitor expects:



\- Easy navigation

\- Mobile-friendly website

\- Fast loading pages

\- Clear product information

\- Multiple contact options

\- Trustworthy brand

\# CRM Implications



Each lead should capture:



\- Customer Type

\- Product Interest

\- Purchase Intent

\- Preferred Contact Method

\- Lead Priority



This allows the sales team to personalize follow-up and improve conversion.
