\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\# System Architecture



\---



\# Purpose



This document describes the overall architecture of the Royal Bhavana Rice Company Lead Management Platform.



The architecture is designed using free-tier technologies while ensuring future scalability with minimal redevelopment.
