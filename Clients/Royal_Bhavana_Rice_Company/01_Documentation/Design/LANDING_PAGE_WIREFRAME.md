\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\# Landing Page Wireframe



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the layout, content flow, and business objective of every section on the Royal Bhavana Rice Company Digital Sales \& Lead Management Platform.



The landing page is designed to educate visitors, build trust, generate enquiries, and increase online product sales.
