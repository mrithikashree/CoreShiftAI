\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\# Information Architecture (IA)



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the structure and flow of information presented on the Royal Bhavana Rice Company Digital Sales \& Lead Management Platform.



The objective is to ensure visitors receive the right information at the right time, leading to higher trust, better engagement, and improved conversions.



\---



\# Information Flow



Visitor Arrives



↓



Understand the Brand



↓



Explore Products



↓



Build Trust



↓



Understand Benefits



↓



Choose an Action



↓



Become a Customer
