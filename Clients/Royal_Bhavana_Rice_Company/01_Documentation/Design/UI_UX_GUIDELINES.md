\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\# UI / UX Guidelines



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the visual and interaction standards for the Royal Bhavana Rice Company Digital Sales \& Lead Management Platform.



The objective is to provide a clean, modern, mobile-first experience that builds trust and encourages customer action.



\---



\# Design Philosophy



Simple.



Professional.



Trustworthy.



Fast.



Mobile First.



Conversion Focused.



\---



\# Design Principles



\- Show important information first.

\- Keep navigation simple.

\- Reduce customer effort.

\- Build trust before asking for action.

\- Keep every screen clean and uncluttered.

\- Ensure accessibility and readability.



\---



\# Responsive Design

Floating Button



Purpose



Quick WhatsApp access.

\---



\# Cards



Use cards for:



\- Products

\- Testimonials

\- Benefits

\- Marketplace Links



Card Rules



\- Rounded corners

\- Soft shadows

\- Equal spacing

\- Consistent padding



\---



\# Forms



Keep forms short.



Maximum 5–6 visible fields.



Show clear validation messages.



Use large touch-friendly controls.



\---



\# Images



Use:



\- High-quality product images

\- Lifestyle images where appropriate

\- Optimized file sizes

\- Consistent aspect ratios



\---



\# Icons



Use simple outline icons.



Keep style consistent.



\---



\# Typography



Heading Hierarchy



H1



Main Page Heading



H2



Section Heading



H3



Card Heading



Body Text



Simple and easy to read.



\---



\# Spacing



Maintain generous white space.



Use consistent margins and padding.



Avoid crowded layouts.



\---



\# Accessibility



Good color contrast.



Readable font sizes.



Clickable buttons.



Keyboard-friendly navigation (future).



\---



\# Performance



Optimize images.



Minimize unnecessary animations.



Prioritize fast page loading.



\---



\# UX Goals



Visitors should be able to:



\- Understand the brand quickly.

\- Find products easily.

\- Contact the company without confusion.

\- Buy online in a few clicks.

\- Submit an enquiry effortlessly.



\---



\# Success Principle



A visitor should know:



Who we are.



What we sell.



Why they should trust us.



How to buy.



How to contact us.



Within the first minute.
