\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\# CRM UI Wireframe



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the layout and workflow of the Google Sheets based CRM used by the Royal Bhavana Rice Company sales team.



The CRM is designed to track enquiries, manage follow-ups, monitor conversions, and support business reporting.



\---



\# CRM Overview



The CRM consists of five primary modules:



1\. Dashboard

2\. Lead Database

3\. Follow-up Tracker

4\. Reports

5\. Settings



\---



\# Dashboard



Purpose



Provide management with a quick overview of business performance.



Widgets



\- Total Leads

\- New Leads Today

\- Hot Leads

\- Warm Leads

\- Cold Leads

\- Follow-ups Due Today

\- Converted Customers

\- Lost Leads
