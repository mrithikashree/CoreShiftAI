Client discussions, decisions, action items

Every client interaction should be documented.

Example:

2026-07-09_Client_Meeting.md

Agenda:
- Landing page approved
- CRM discussed

Decisions:
- Multiple product enquiry
- Hot/Warm/Cold lead priority

Action Items:
- Client to send product images
- CoreShift AI to build Sprint 1

This habit becomes invaluable months later when someone asks, "Why did we implement it this way?"
