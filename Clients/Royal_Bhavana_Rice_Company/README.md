\# Royal Bhavana Rice Company

\## Lead Generation \& Business Automation Platform



\---



\## Project Overview



The Royal Bhavana Rice Company Lead Generation \& Business Automation Platform is a centralized digital solution designed to capture customer enquiries, manage leads, improve follow-ups, and provide business insights through dashboards and reporting.



The platform is being developed by CoreShift AI using modern cloud technologies with scalability, automation, and future AI integration in mind.



\---



\## Business Objectives



\- Increase lead generation

\- Centralize customer enquiries

\- Improve follow-up management

\- Reduce manual work

\- Improve customer conversion

\- Build a scalable CRM platform

\- Prepare for future AI automation
